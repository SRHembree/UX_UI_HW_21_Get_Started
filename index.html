Getting Started: Understanding the 5 W's (And 'H') of Travel



Where to Go

What kind of adventure are you looking for? Beach, culture, food? Pick a location and start from there.

Who to Bring Along

Party members make or break a trip, so choose travel companions wisely. Also, plan for interests you all share.

How Much to Spend

Are you traveling on a manageable budget or can you splurge on fine food or souvenirs?

What to Bring with You

Arriving without underwear is a cardinal sin (and they may not carry your size in stores). Make sure to pack essentials for where you'll be.

When is the Trip

Weather will fluctuate, eateries will close during holidays, and those jeans will eventually start to smell without a wash. Plan out outfits ahead of time.

Why You Should Look Ahead

Festivals could be going on nearby. The Queen may be coming to town and traffic will back up. You could give cookies to a deer. Know before you go.
